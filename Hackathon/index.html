About Kwality Wall's

Our Story

Kwality Wall's has been bringing joy to millions of Indians since 1956. Our journey began with a simple mission: to create the finest quality ice cream using the best ingredients.

Over the decades, we've introduced innovative products that have become household favorites, from the iconic Cornetto to the delightful Feast.

Today, we continue to innovate while staying true to our commitment to quality, bringing smiles to faces across the country.
